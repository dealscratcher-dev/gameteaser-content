Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 3, 2026

Season Rush is a fan-made countdown site for mobile game seasons. This page explains what data is used.

Data stored on your device

Season end dates you edit and daily “I played today” streaks are saved in localStorage only. We do not run user accounts or a backend database.

Google AdSense & cookies

This site monetizes through Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies to serve and measure ads, including personalized ads where allowed by law.

When you click “OK, show ads” on the banner, we remember that choice in localStorage as seasonRushAdConsent.
